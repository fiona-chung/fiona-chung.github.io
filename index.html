Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds of golden or red beets, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces or wedges
  • 4 teaspoons of extra-virgin olive oil or canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh or 2 teaspoons dried herbs, such as marjoram, oregano and/or rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon of freshly grated lemon zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of freshly ground pepper
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Nutrition

  • 118 calories
  • 5g fat
  • 17g carbs
  • 3g protein

Lemon-Herb Roasted Beets

Total Cook Time: 30 minutes

Serves 4

Step 1.

Position rack in lower third of oven; preheat to 450 degrees F.

Step 2.

Combine oil, herbs, lemon zest, salt and pepper in a large bowl. Add beets; toss to coat with the seasoning mixture.

Step 3.

Spread the beets evenly on a rimmed baking sheet.

Step 4.

Roast, stirring once or twice, until the beets are tender and browned, 20 to 25 minutes. Toss the roasted vegetables with lemon juice.
